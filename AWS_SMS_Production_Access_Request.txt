STEPS TO GET SMS PRODUCTION ACCESS (AWS)
=========================================

STEP 1 — Open this link in your browser:
https://support.console.aws.amazon.com/support/home#/case/create?issueType=service-limit-increase


STEP 2 — Fill out the form exactly like this:

  • Service:        Service Quotas
  • Category:       AWS End User Messaging SMS (Pinpoint)
  • Severity:       General Limits
  • Region:         us-east-1
  • Resource Type:  General Limits
  • Quota:          SMS Production Access
  • New quota value: 1


STEP 3 — In the Description box, copy and paste this:

------------------------------------------------------------------
We are CMP Logistics, a freight and trucking dispatch company.
We use AWS SNS/Pinpoint to send transactional SMS messages to
truck drivers containing their load details and a GPS tracking
link. Messages are sent only to our own employed drivers in the
US and Canada. Drivers opt-in by being onboarded as employees.
We do not send any promotional or marketing messages.

Website: https://main.dkyps2bncnu4g.amplifyapp.com
Message type: Transactional
Countries: United States and Canada
Opt-in process: Employee onboarding
------------------------------------------------------------------


STEP 4 — Choose your preferred language and click Submit.


NOTES:
  - AWS typically responds within 24 hours.
  - Make sure you have sent at least one test SMS from your
    account before submitting (you likely already have).
  - If you use multiple AWS regions, submit a separate
    request for each one.
